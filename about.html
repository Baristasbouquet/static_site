A little about us and a few answers to common questions:

We're a local, family-owned, independent business shining a spotlight on our local community centered at 60th St and Ashworth Rd in West Des Moines, IA. We feature awesome baked goods from The Bake Shoppe, the best beans from Pammel Park Coffee CO, and flowers from several local farms in season. We're open 7am - 2pm 6 days a week (every day except Tuesday, those are for tacos!).

One day Brandt and Stacey came up with the idea of a coffee/flower shop. It melded their respective worlds together under one roof and lead to about 30 seconds of blissfull thinking that they were on to something new and uniqiue. A quick google search shattered that idea but further discussion cemented the power of the concept and how it could be used to help contribute to the local community. We take quality crafted products from local businesses very seriously and bring that focus to West Des Moines, IA.

A note about sustainability- We make a big deal about trying to be good stewards of our community and that extends to this earth. Every single-use product we serve our products in is biodegradable or compostable. We also try to stick with reusable materials when possible like glasses in house or (shameless plug warning!) offering growelettes to take home cold brew. We're NOT perfect in this regard (or any others for that matter) but we appreciate your help in making the effort!

FAQ's

What sizes do you offer? - One! We offer one size designed to be as generous as correct proportions will allow.

Do you offer alternative milks? - We offer oat milk at no additional charge.

Can I get an extra shot with that? - Yes, BUT. . . We talk a lot of folks out of adding a shot to our espresso drinks because our standard shot is actually a very generous double and so you end up with a very strong drink. Matcha or Chai are always good with espresso though (in our opinion).

Do you deliver? - No, we do not offer delivery at this time.

Are you hiring? - Check our team page for postings or details

Do you do custom arrangements? - Absolutely! Reach out to us and we'll be happy to put something special together for you!

Do you offer subscriptions for flowers or plants? - Absolutely! For more information about our floral subscription packages please check our subscriptions page.

Why are you closed on Tuesdays? - Tuesdays are for Tacos! We also hope our team gets to recharge, spend time with family, or do whatever it is they like to on our only closed day of the week. We realize this may not seem conventional to many but it is our day of rest that fits business needs best. Also, we REALLY do like tacos a lot!
