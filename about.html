A little about us and a few answers to common questions:

We're a local, family-owned, independent business shining a spotlight on the local communities we're a part of. We feature awesome baked goods we make in-house, the best beans from Pammel Park Coffee CO, and flowers from several local farms in season. We're open every day of the week; 7am - 2pm Monday through Thursday and 7am - 3pm Friday, Saturday, and Sunday.

One day Brandt and Stacey came up with the idea of a coffee/flower shop. It melded their respective worlds together under one roof and lead to about 30 seconds of blissfull thinking that they were on to something new and uniqiue. A quick google search shattered that idea but further discussion cemented the power of the concept and how it could be used to help contribute to the local community. We make quality crafted products in-house and also feature art and goods from local vendors.

A note about sustainability- We make a big deal about trying to be good stewards of our community and that extends to this earth. Every single-use product we serve our products in is biodegradable or compostable. We also try to stick with reusable materials when possible like glasses in house or (shameless plug warning!) offering growelettes to take home cold brew. We're NOT perfect in this regard (or any others for that matter) but we appreciate your help in making the effort!

FAQ's

What sizes do you offer? - One! We offer one size designed to be as generous as correct proportions will allow.

Do you offer alternative milks? - We offer oat milk at no additional charge.

Can I get an extra shot with that? - Yes, BUT. . . We talk a lot of folks out of adding a shot to our espresso drinks because our standard shot is actually a very generous double and so you end up with a very strong drink. Matcha or Chai are always good with espresso though (in our opinion).

Do you deliver? - We are now offering local floral delivery thanks to a local courier! Order online here. We don't offer delivery for food & beverage.

Are you hiring? - Check our team page for postings or details

Do you do custom arrangements? - Absolutely! Reach out to us and we'll be happy to put something special together for you! Check out our floral page for more info.

Do you offer subscriptions for flowers or plants? - Absolutely! For more information about our standard floral subscription packages please check out this document. We love custom designing packages to fit different needs so please reach out with any questions or ideas!
