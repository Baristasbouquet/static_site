Coffee | Flowers

In either of our primary passions (see above), we always strive for the same goal: Custom, hand-crafted, and high quality offerings. That means house made syrups that start with turbinado sugar to highlight the freshest beans from our custom blends. Also featuring locally grown flowers in beautiful seasonal arrangements made right here by hand.

We're a local, family-owned, independent business shining a spotlight on our local community centered at 60th St and Ashworth Rd in West Des Moines, IA. We feature awesome baked goods from The Bake Shoppe, the best beans from Pammel Park Coffee CO, and flowers from several local farms in season. We're open 7am - 2pm 6 days a week (every day except Tuesday, those are for tacos!). Stop in, take a sip, smell the flowers!
