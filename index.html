Barista's Bouquet is a coffee shop with flowers (coffee/flower shop) in West Des Moines, Iowa.

Coffee, the good stuff!

We're obsessed and will be offering the very best of what we've found!

That means the best of drip to espresso classics, special house-made syrups, cold-brew, loose-leaf tea. We've got something for the season and others that don't need a special season!

Flowers, for a brighter day!

We'll be featuring unique and beatiful arrangements all custom designed in-house and available for pickup. When feasible our features focus on locally-sourced flowers bound with sustainable products and reclaimed materials.

TL;DR- Hand-tied bouquets, custom arrangements, and unique offerings!
