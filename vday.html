Valentine's Day Online Ordering Here!

Info and offerings:

All orders can be picked up between Wednesday, 2/12 through Friday 2/14 between 7am-3pm as booked at checkout. For other circumstances please contact an associate! All offerings are hand picked and designed by our very own floral fashoinista and showcase a fresh take on v-day classics. Online ordering is encouraged but you can also book your florals in store.
Be Mine Bouquet
All offerings are hand-picked and designed in-house. Be Mine features 10-12 Valentine's themed blooms in a custom bouquet with fresh accents hand wrapped (no vase) for your Valentine.
Beloved Bouquet
Hand-picked and designed in-house. An even larger fresh take on 12-15 Valentine's themed blooms hand-wrapped (no vase) in an extravagant bouquet.
Affection Arrangement
Hand-picked and designed in-house. A frosted pink Brooklyn vase adorned with 10-12 Valentine's themed blooms and fresh accents to create a stunning arrangement.
Adoration Arrangement
Hand-picked and designed in-house. This larger arrangement adds a classy touch with fresh accents and 12-15 Valentine's themed blooms in a Gera Compote Vase.
Amour Arrangement
Our most extravagant offering of the season, hand-picked and designed as well. A grand and classy arrangement built into a coral Belini vase with 18-22 Valentine's themed blooms and fresh accents. Oozes affection in an elegant way.
J'Adore Rose Dozen Arrangement
A fresh take on the V-day classic; a dozen roses. As ever, hand-picked and designed in house. Fresh accents and greenery further the appeal of a dozen beautiful Valentine's themed roses built into a mint Roseland vase.
