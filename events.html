Flu Clinic!

Friday 12/6: 7am - 10am

Still need a flu shot for the season? We're partnering with Hy-Vee to make that happen! From 7am-9am on Friday, Dec. 6th - we'll have a pharmacist at the shop to help you be better prepared for the cold season.

Bring your insurance card and your Hy-Vee Fuel Saver card to get your 20 cent fuel saver. If you don't have insurance, no problem. Flu shots without insurance are $44. This Clinic will only be for people who are 12 years and older.

Reach out to us with any questions! **Under 19 with medicaid will be available for cash price only
