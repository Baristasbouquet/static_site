Upcoming Events at Barista's Bouquet!


Check back here for basic information about upcoming events. You can also check out the event on Facebook via the link.

It's our goal to be a space for our community and we hold many types of events in our space with that in mind! If you have ideas for a public event, questions about a private event, or anything related please reach out to us directly.

Pumpkin Succulent class. Gourd-geous!

Sunday 10/5 at 3:30pm

Join us for a hands-on fall workshop where you'll turn pumpkins into living works of art! Choose between one large pumpkin or a trio of minis, then decorate with lush succulents, moss, and seasonal accents. We'll guide you step-by-step in creating your own unique centerpiece that lasts all season long.

All materials are included—pumpkins, succulents, moss, and use of tools—plus a cozy drink of your choice to sip while you create. Perfect for a festive outing with friends, family, or just a little creative “you time.”

All ages welcome (participants must be comfortable using clippers and hot glue) $40 per person or $75 and bring a friend for 2 tickets.

Plant Swap!

Saturday 10/18 from 8am-2pm

Got some plants, clippings, or seeds itching for a new home? Or perhaps you've got a plant that's flopped and needs a superhero to save it? Swing by Barista's Bouquet on Saturday, October 18th from 8am-2pm for our Plant Swap!

Here's the scoop: - Bring a clipping or propagation to share the green love! - Got seeds? Bring 'em along! - Have a healthy plant you're eager to gift? Yes, please! - Have a plant that's looking a bit worse for wear? Bring it - it might just find its plant soulmate! - Jot down your plant's name for its fresh new plant parent, so they can shower it with love and keep it thriving!

You can bring as many as your green thumb desires, but we recommend the golden rule: Bring 1 plant + Take 1 plant, so everyone leaves with a leafy new friend! This is a free event!
