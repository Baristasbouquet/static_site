Coffee with Chrysalis
West Des Moines Location

Friday 1/30 | 8am

Coffee with Chrysalis is a series of free, informal events designed to educate and engage the community on timely issues affecting women and girls in Greater Des Moines. Led by local experts, these discussions provide valuable insights and foster dialogue on topics ranging from education and health to social justice and leadership.

This gathering will be joined by staff from Healthy Birth Day, who will share information about the Count the Kicks app and how it supports healthy pregnancies.
